Shakshuka

Description

Moroccan shakshuka is a delicious North African breakfast of poached eggs in spicy tomato sauce. Make this recipe for an easy weekend brunch!

Ingredients

  • Saffron threads
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato paste
  • Ground cumin
  • Bell peppers
  • Minced garlic
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • 8 large eggs

Steps

  1. Bloom the saffron threads.
  2. Cook the vegetables.
  3. Poach the eggs.
  4. Garnish and serve.
